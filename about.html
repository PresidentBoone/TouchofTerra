Our Story & Mission

Discover the heart behind Touch of Terra and our commitment to creating lasting change

Our Mission

Touch of Terra exists to provide immediate relief to those experiencing homelessness through our backpack initiative, while simultaneously working to educate our community and advocate for systemic solutions that address the root causes of homelessness.

We believe that every person deserves dignity, compassion, and the basic necessities for survival. Our mission is to bridge the gap between immediate need and long-term change, creating a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

The Backpack Initiative

What's Inside Each Backpack

Every backpack we distribute is thoughtfully packed with essential items that provide immediate relief and dignity to those in need. We focus on items that are practical, durable, and show that someone cares.

Food Items

Non-perishable meals, snacks, and nutrition bars

Hygiene Products

Soap, toothbrush, toothpaste, shampoo, and sanitizer

Clothing

Socks, underwear, and weather-appropriate items

First Aid

Band-aids, antiseptic, pain relief medication

Resources

Local service guides and emergency contacts

Personal Touch

Handwritten notes of encouragement and hope

Carrying Hope & Essentials

Advocacy & Education

While we provide immediate relief, we recognize that lasting change requires addressing the systemic issues that contribute to homelessness. Our advocacy work focuses on education, awareness, and policy change.

Community Education

We host workshops, presentations, and community discussions to help people understand the complex realities of homelessness, breaking down stereotypes and building empathy through shared stories and facts.

Policy Advocacy

We work with local government officials, community leaders, and organizations to advocate for policies that address housing affordability, mental health services, and job training programs.

Community Partnerships

We collaborate with shelters, food banks, healthcare providers, and social services to create a comprehensive support network that addresses multiple aspects of homelessness.

Legacy of Terra

Terra was more than just the inspiration for our name—she was the embodiment of compassion, service, and unwavering belief in the inherent worth of every human being. Her life was a testament to the power of small acts of kindness and their ability to create ripples of positive change.

Though Terra is no longer with us physically, her spirit lives on in every backpack we prepare, every hand we extend in friendship, and every voice we raise in advocacy. She taught us that true wealth isn't measured in material possessions, but in the lives we touch and the love we share.

"The earth does not belong to us; we belong to the earth. All things are connected like the blood that unites one family. What we do to the earth, we do to ourselves."

— In loving memory of Terra, whose spirit guides every step of our journey.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a community where homelessness is rare, brief, and non-recurring. Our long-term goals focus on prevention, rapid rehousing, and comprehensive support systems.

Housing First

Advocate for and support housing-first policies that prioritize getting people into stable housing quickly, then providing support services.

Education & Training

Develop programs that provide job training, life skills, and educational opportunities to help people achieve long-term stability.

Mental Health Support

Expand access to mental health and addiction services, recognizing these as health issues that require compassionate treatment.

Community Building

Foster a community culture of inclusion, understanding, and mutual support where everyone has value and opportunity.

Our Journey

2022
The Vision Begins
After losing Terra, DyLon felt called to honor her memory through meaningful action. The idea of providing backpacks filled with essentials was born.
Early 2023
First Backpacks
The first 25 backpacks were assembled and distributed in downtown Louisville, marking the humble beginning of Touch of Terra's mission.
Mid 2023
Growing Community
Word spread about the initiative, and community members began volunteering and donating supplies. The first regular volunteers joined the mission.
Late 2023
Official Organization
Touch of Terra became an official nonprofit organization, enabling broader fundraising efforts and community partnerships.
2024
Expanding Impact
Partnerships with local shelters and service providers were established. Educational workshops and advocacy efforts began in earnest.
Today
Growing Movement
With over 500 backpacks distributed and growing community support, Touch of Terra continues to expand its reach while staying true to Terra's legacy of compassion.
