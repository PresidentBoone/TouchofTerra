Our Story & Mission

Discover the heart behind Touch of Terra and our commitment to creating lasting change

Our Mission

Touch of Terra was founded to provide immediate relief to those experiencing homelessness through our backpack initiative, while simultaneously working to educate our community and advocate for systemic solutions that address the root causes of homelessness.

We believe that every person deserves dignity, compassion, and the basic necessities to sustain themselves. Our mission is to bridge the gap between immediate need and long-term change, creating a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

The Backpack Initiative

What's Inside Each Backpack

Every backpack we distribute is thoughtfully packed with essential items that provide immediate relief and dignity to those in need. We focus on items that are practical, durable, and show that someone cares.

Food Items

Non-perishable meals, snacks, and nutrition bars

Hygiene Products

Soap, toothbrush, toothpaste, shampoo, and sanitizer

Clothing

Socks, underwear, and weather-appropriate items

First Aid

Band-aids, antiseptic, pain relief medication

Resources

Local service guides and emergency contacts

Personal Touch

Handwritten notes of encouragement and hope

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a community where homelessness is rare, brief, and non-recurring. Our long-term goals focus on prevention, rapid rehousing, and comprehensive support systems.

Housing First

Advocate for and support housing-first policies that prioritize getting people into stable housing quickly, then providing support services.

Education & Training

Develop programs that provide job training, life skills, and educational opportunities to help people achieve long-term stability.

Mental Health Support

Expand access to mental health and addiction services, recognizing these as health issues that require compassionate treatment.

Community Building

Foster a community culture of inclusion, understanding, and mutual support where everyone has value and opportunity.

Our Journey

2022

The Vision Begins

After losing Terra, DyLon felt called to honor her memory through meaningful action. The idea of providing backpacks filled with essentials was born.

Early 2023

First Backpacks

The first 25 backpacks were assembled and distributed in downtown Louisville, marking the humble beginning of Touch of Terra's mission.

Mid 2023

Growing Community

Word spread about the initiative, and community members began volunteering and donating supplies. The first regular volunteers joined the mission.

Late 2023

Official Organization

Touch of Terra became an official nonprofit organization, enabling broader fundraising efforts and community partnerships.

2024

Expanding Impact

Partnerships with local shelters and service providers were established. Educational workshops and advocacy efforts began in earnest.

Today

Growing Movement

With over 500 backpacks distributed and growing community support, Touch of Terra continues to expand its reach while staying true to Terra's legacy of compassion.

Touch of Terra

Carrying compassion, one backpack at a time. Providing essential supplies to those experiencing homelessness while advocating for lasting solutions.
