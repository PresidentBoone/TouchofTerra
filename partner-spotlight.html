Saint Xavier High School

Founding Partner since 2024

Saint Xavier High School is a Catholic, college preparatory school for young men located in Louisville, Kentucky. As one of our founding partners, St. X has been instrumental in Touch of Terra's mission since day one. Their students, faculty, and alumni have shown incredible dedication to serving those experiencing homelessness in our community.

Partnership Contributions:

  • Student volunteers for backpack distribution events
  • Annual service projects and fundraising campaigns
  • Leadership in community outreach initiatives
  • Donated supplies and resources for backpack assembly

Wayside Christian Mission

Distribution Partner since 2024

Wayside Christian Mission is Louisville's largest and oldest emergency homeless shelter, providing shelter, meals, and supportive services to men, women, and children experiencing homelessness. As our key distribution partner, Wayside helps us reach those who need our backpacks most.

Partnership Contributions:

  • Primary distribution location for Touch of Terra backpacks
  • Connecting us with individuals and families in need
  • Providing facility space for backpack assembly events
  • Sharing valuable insights on community needs
