Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes! Touch of Terra is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax deductible to the full extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your records.

How secure is my donation?

Your donation is processed through Zeffy, a secure platform that uses SSL encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and financial information.

How much of my donation goes to the cause?

100% of your donation goes directly to our mission. Zeffy covers their processing fees through optional tips from donors, ensuring every dollar you donate reaches those in need.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes! You can set up monthly recurring donations through our Zeffy form. This helps us plan ahead and ensures a steady supply of backpacks for those in need.

How can I see the impact of my donation?

We regularly share updates on our social media and through our newsletter about the backpacks we've distributed and the lives we've touched. You'll see exactly how your generosity makes a difference.

Are there other ways to help besides donating money?

Absolutely! You can volunteer to help pack backpacks, donate supplies directly, or help spread awareness about our mission. Visit our volunteer page to learn more.
