Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering with Touch of Terra is more than giving back—it's about building community, creating connections, and being part of meaningful change.

Make a Direct Impact

See the immediate difference your efforts make in someone's day. Every backpack you help prepare provides essential supplies and shows someone that their community cares.

Build Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for helping others. Our volunteer community is supportive, welcoming, and committed to positive change.

Learn & Grow

Gain new perspectives on homelessness and social justice while developing skills in community organizing, advocacy, and compassionate service.

Flexible Commitment

We offer opportunities that fit various schedules and availability. Whether you have an hour a week or a day a month, there's a way to contribute.

Educational Experience

Learn about the root causes of homelessness and become an advocate for systemic solutions in your community and beyond.

Personal Fulfillment

Experience the joy and satisfaction that comes from knowing you're making a positive difference in someone's life and in your community.

Volunteer Opportunities

Backpack Preparation

2-4 hours monthly

Join our core mission by helping to assemble backpacks filled with essential items. This involves sorting donations, packing supplies, and adding personal notes of encouragement.

What You'll Do:

  • Sort and organize donated items
  • Pack backpacks with care and attention
  • Write encouraging notes to recipients
  • Quality check completed backpacks

Distribution & Outreach

3-5 hours monthly

Be the face of Touch of Terra by distributing backpacks directly to those in need. This role involves compassionate interaction and connecting people with additional resources.

What You'll Do:

  • Distribute backpacks in designated areas
  • Engage respectfully with recipients
  • Provide information about local services
  • Document impact for our records

Event Support

4-8 hours per event

Help organize and run fundraising events, community education workshops, and awareness campaigns. Perfect for those who enjoy event planning and public engagement.

What You'll Do:

  • Assist with event setup and breakdown
  • Welcome and engage with attendees
  • Help with registration and materials
  • Support speakers and presentations

Advocacy & Education

Flexible schedule

Join our advocacy efforts by helping with research, community education, and policy initiatives. Ideal for those passionate about systemic change and social justice.

What You'll Do:

  • Research homelessness issues and solutions
  • Help create educational materials
  • Attend community meetings
  • Support policy advocacy efforts

Digital & Marketing

2-6 hours monthly

Use your creative and technical skills to help spread our message. Manage social media, create content, design graphics, or help with our website and digital presence.

What You'll Do:

  • Manage social media accounts
  • Create engaging content and graphics
  • Help with website updates
  • Document our impact through photos/video

Administrative Support

3-5 hours monthly

Help keep Touch of Terra running smoothly with behind-the-scenes support. Perfect for those who are organized, detail-oriented, and want to contribute valuable operational help.

What You'll Do:

  • Assist with volunteer coordination
  • Help with data entry and record keeping
  • Support fundraising administration
  • Organize supplies and inventory
