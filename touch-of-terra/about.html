Our Mission

Touch of Terra provides ready-to-go backpacks filled with food, toiletries, and other necessities for those in need, while also educating the public and advocating for solutions to homelessness.

Backpack Initiative

Each backpack contains essential items such as food, hygiene products, and other necessities to support individuals and families in need.

Advocacy & Education

We believe in raising awareness about homelessness and advocating for sustainable solutions to this pressing issue.

Legacy of Terra

Terra's legacy inspires us to carry on her mission of compassion and support for those in need.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a world where everyone has access to basic necessities and support, fostering a community of care and compassion.
